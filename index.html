Our Services

Property Accounting

We handle the day-to-day financial transactions of your properties, ensuring accurate record-keeping and financial health. This includes the recording of accounts payable, accounts receivable, bank reconciliations, and the monthly close process.

Financial Reporting

We generate clear and concise financial statements so you can make informed decisions based on the performance of your real estate investments.

Historical Accounting/Backbooks

We catch up on your neglected bookkeeping and get your bank reconciliations and real estate accounting records up-to-date.

Lease Abstracts

We summarize key terms and critical dates of your commercial real estate leases, ensuring you understand your obligations and those of your tenants.

CAM Reconciliations

We ensure your common area maintenance expenses for your properties are accurately recorded and billed to your tenants in accordance with their lease agreements.

Software Migrations

We seamlessly transer your real estate accounting data to a new system, minimizing downtime and maximizing efficiency.
